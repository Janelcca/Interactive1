The Mission District

An interesting hipster neighborhood
that is fun to stroll around in. It is full of graffiti, street art,
interesting people, shops, cafes, restaurants, and boutiques. My daughter and I hang out in this neighborhood a lot.
They have a fabulous farmers market and the best food.

One of our favorite resturants is MYRIAD. It serves globally inspired food and drinks in The Mission
in a relaxed and kid friendly environment. It has a hip vibe with a long wait but great service.
It is a modern mission resturant(genterfication) and the mission is my favorite neighborhood in San Francisco
