The Mission District

An interesting hipster neighborhood that is fun to stroll around in. It is full of graffiti, street art, interesting people, shops, cafes, restaurants, and boutiques. My daughter and I hang out in this neighborhood a lot. They have a fabulous farmers market and the best food.
One of our favorite resturants is MYRIAD. It serves globally inspired food and drinks in The Mission in a relaxed and kid friendly environment. It has a hip vibe with a long wait but great service. It is a modern mission resturant(genterfication) and the mission is my favorite neighborhood in San Francisco

I live in Bernal heights which is about a 10 to 15 minute walk.

Mission Dolores Park

Mission Dolores Park is one of my favorite parks in the mission. The mission is also always warmer than the rest of San Francisco, There are so many parks to choose from this is my daughters favorite place to play.It is close to Biright (fav grocery store) and Ice Cream!
